ETH Wallet

Generate a Cloud Wallet

Selecting this option will create a custodial ethereum wallet where the private keys are stored on the cloud and all the operations are triggered from the Lazarus Network Application Servers.

Link Existing Wallet

Link your existing ETH wallet with the Lazarus Network Account and take control of all your Blockchain Transactions. Enter your Seed Words to load your Wallet Address.
